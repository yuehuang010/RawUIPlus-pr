# RawUI Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-22_

RawUI ("the app") is a desktop photo editor that runs entirely on your
Windows PC.

## Data we collect

The app collects **no personal data**. Specifically, RawUI:

- Does not create an account or require sign-in.
- Does not transmit your photos or any usage data over the internet.
- Does not include analytics, advertising, or tracking.
- Does not access your location, camera, microphone, or contacts.

## How your files are used

RawUI reads the photo files you open and writes the JPEG files you export,
only to the folders you choose. All image processing happens locally on your
device. The app also stores its own working data on your PC (for example, an
image cache and edit history) to make editing faster; this data never leaves
your computer.

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.
